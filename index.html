What is Human-Centric Cybersecurity?

Focus on People

It's an approach that places people—their behaviors, vulnerabilities, and strengths—at the center of the security strategy, rather than focusing purely on technology.

Building Resilience

By understanding human factors, we create systems that are not just secure, but also intuitive, usable, and resilient against manipulation and social engineering.
